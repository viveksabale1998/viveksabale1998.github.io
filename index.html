Welcome

I am a quantum computing researcher at IIT Jodhpur, specializing in quantum information and computation, open quantum systems, and quantum machine learning. My research involves the use of advanced computational methods for quantum as well as classical simulations of open quantum system (OQS) dynamics. I also employ techniques including tensor network methods, variational quantum algorithms, and quantum simulations to investigate quantum correlations, ergotropy, and non-Markovian dynamics.

I work extensively with Julia, Python, and Mathematica, developing high-performance implementations for simulations of complex quantum systems dynamics.

Research Interests

  • Quantum entanglement
  • Open quantum systems
  • Quantum batteries
  • Tensor network methods
  • Quantum computing
  • Quantum machine learning
